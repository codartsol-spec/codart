Who We Are

We Turn Vision Into Digital Reality

CODART started as a simple idea between three friends — Oneeb, Anas, and Hassan — who shared a passion for web development, graphic design, and building things that look great online. What began as a side hustle quickly turned into a proper freelance studio with real clients across Japan, the US, and beyond.

We don't operate like a big agency — we work closely with every client, treat each project personally, and bring the same hustle we had on day one. When you work with CODART, you work directly with the people doing the work.
